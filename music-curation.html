SECTION 8: Soul Sounds

I played a key role in a collaboration with Melbourne's iconic venue Section 8 and DJ Shoges (Vapour Recordings), serving as creative lead. This involved designing all promotional materials, curating and performing an opening 3-hour soulful house B3B set that bridged lounge with dancefloor moods, and managing guest outreach. The result was a significant boost in attendance, transforming a standard Wednesday into a night rivaling Saturday's crowds.
